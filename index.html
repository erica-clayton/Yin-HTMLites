Go any direction on the map from Nashville and you can find a number of great weekend destinations. No matter if you want to take a short day trip to somewhere new, do the seventh inning stretch at a Major League Baseball game, experience breathtaking views of the mountains, or just get away to a hotel for the weekend and eat your way through some of the local hotspots -- we've got you covered!
